Instructions

  1. Press any button to Start your game.

  2. Press Up-Arrowkey for slowing your tetris.

  3. Press Down-Arrowkey for faster moving your tetris(faster Downward movement).

  4. Press Left-Arrowkey for moving your tetris Left(Leftward movement).

  5. Press Right-Arrowkey for moving your Right(rightward movement)

  6. Don't touch the upper border of box.In case if you touched your Game will be over.

  7. press Q and W button for changing shape of tetrix.

  8. If any of your Row is fullyFilled ,your score will increase as well as your 1 row will vanishes
