Instructions

  1. Press any button to Start your game.

  2. Press Left-Arrowkey for moving your ball Left(Leftward movement).

  3. Press Up-Arrowkey for moving your ball Right(upward movement)

  4. Don't touch the upper border of box.In case if you touched border your Game will be over.

  5. For securing more and more score continue moving toward hole to go down.
