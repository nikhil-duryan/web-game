Instructions

  1. Press any button to Start your game.

  2. Press Up-Arrowkey for moving your Snake Up(upward movement).

  3. Press Down-Arrowkey for moving your Snake Down(Downward movement).

  4. Press Left-Arrowkey for moving your Snake Left(Leftward movement).

  5. Press right-Arrowkey for moving your Snake Right(rightward movement)

  6. Don't touch the border of box.In case if you touched border your Game will be over.

  7. Don't touch the body of snake.In case if you touched border your Game will be over.
